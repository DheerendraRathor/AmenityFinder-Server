About
How many times have you cursed that last can of soft drink while travelling someplace new? How many times have you franctically asked random shopkeepers about the nearest place to relieve yourself?

No more! We now have a one stop solution for all your wee-woes!

Locate the nearest washroom, or add a new one. Oh but wait. What if the nearest washroom isn't in a good state? Well, find the highest rated one. Rate washrooms, read and post comments about them and help others who might be in need.

All this in a single clean interface with no ads of any kind!
